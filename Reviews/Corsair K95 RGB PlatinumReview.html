Design and Durability:

Aircraft Grade Anodized Brushed Aluminum Frame: At the core of the Corsair K95 RGB lies an exquisite aircraft-grade anodized brushed aluminum frame. This premium construction not only elevates the keyboard's visual appeal but also ensures a durable and lightweight foundation. Crafted to withstand the rigors of extensive gaming, the aluminum frame guarantees longevity, making the K95 RGB a reliable companion for a lifetime of gaming adventures.

Performance:

100% Cherry MX Speed RGB Mechanical Key Switches: Powering the Corsair K95 RGB are the renowned Cherry MX Speed RGB mechanical key switches. Boasting a remarkable 1.2mm actuation point and gold contact construction, these switches deliver blistering speed and unrivaled durability. The result is a keyboard that responds with lightning-fast precision, providing gamers with a competitive edge in high-stakes environments.

6 Programmable G Keys for In-Game Macros: For the tactically inclined, the K95 RGB offers six programmable G keys. These dedicated macro keys empower users to assign single keystrokes or intricate multi-key combinations, strategically enhancing their in-game capabilities. With full key (NKRO) and 100% anti-ghosting, every command is executed seamlessly, ensuring an uninterrupted gaming experience.

HID Keyboard Report Rate: 1000 Hz: The Corsair K95 RGB sets a high standard for responsiveness with its HID keyboard report rate of 1000 Hz. This ensures that every keystroke is transmitted to the computer at an astonishingly rapid rate, minimizing input lag and providing gamers with a fluid and instantaneous connection to their virtual worlds.

Customization and Lighting:

Dynamic Multicolor Per Key Backlighting with Light Edge: Immerse yourself in a visual spectacle with the Corsair K95 RGB's dynamic multicolor per-key backlighting. Each key can be individually customized for color and illumination level, allowing users to create a personalized gaming environment. Additionally, the 19 zones on the top edge light bar contribute to sophisticated and dramatic animations, adding an extra layer of immersion to your gaming setup.

Storage and Convenience:

8MB Profile Storage with Hardware Macro and Lighting Playback: The K95 RGB provides the convenience of 8MB profile storage, allowing users to save up to three profiles directly on the keyboard. This feature grants on-the-go access to personalized settings, independent of external software. Compatible with Windows 10, Windows 8, Windows 7, or Windows Vista, the K95 RGB ensures broad compatibility, making it a versatile choice for users across different operating systems.

Conclusion

the Corsair K95 RGB stands as a pinnacle of gaming keyboards, offering a perfect synergy of durability, performance, and customization. Tailored for avid gamers and enthusiasts, its aircraft-grade aluminum frame, Cherry MX Speed RGB switches, and extensive customization options make it an ideal choice for those seeking a premium gaming experience.
